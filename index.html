Studio Victoria

Where your dreams become art

Who we are:

The Studio Victoria was founded in 1998 in Dublin serving artists with recording and audio editions. Later it was included services of video recording and editing as also events coverage with audio and video equipments renting.
The Studio Victoria became a reference in the Irish market producing very successful pieces of work together with various artists.

Audio & Video

With the most updated tech, our studio offers Audio and Video creation, editing and special effects for everyone. There is no small job, from a starting Youtube channel to the most famous artists, each work deserves the best.

Studio Rooms

Our Studio Rooms are well equiped and prepared for your use, silent rooms, big or small, we make sure every artist will have everything they need. The artist only needs to book a room and come over to produce their art work.

Events & Equipments

For customers that don't want to deal with the art work, our professionals can help to produce the piece os Audio or Video at the client location. We also rent equipments to artists that take their adventure on the open world.
